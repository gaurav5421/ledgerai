# LedgerAI

AI-powered financial analysis agent that answers questions about public company financials using SEC EDGAR filings.

**Covered companies:** AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN, JPM

**What I can do:**
- Look up financial metrics (revenue, margins, EPS, cash flow, debt)
- Analyze trends across quarters and years
- Compare companies (with appropriate caveats)
- Decompose why a metric changed
- Provide full provenance and confidence scoring

**What I won't do:**
- Investment advice or stock recommendations
- Future projections or forecasts
- Non-financial questions
